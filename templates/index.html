How to Use the App

  • Navigate: Use the menu at the top to access different features of the app.
  • Status: View the live video feed to monitor for any intrusions and app status.
  • Settings: Adjust the detection sensor, camera position and other settings in the settings menu.
  • Contact: Configure contact and alert preferences to receive notifications in case of any detected motion.
  • Signal: Configure signal group and alert preferences to receive notifications in case of any detected motion.
  • History: Check the history section to review past intrusion events and recorded footage.
